Research Novelty

The PWIS Scoring Mechanism

The Phenotype-Weighted Inheritance Scoring Mechanism (PWIS) is the core research contribution of GeneTrace. It quantifies hereditary burden by combining lineage weight, disease severity, and age of onset into a single interpretable score.

  • Parent lineage weight = 1.0 | Grandparent = 0.5
  • Severity: None=0 · Mild=0.25 · Moderate=0.60 · Severe=1.0
  • Early onset (<45 yrs) weighted 1.0 vs late onset 0.3
  • Paternal and maternal scores computed independently
